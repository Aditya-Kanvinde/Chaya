We make Memories

Welcome to Chayachitrikaran. We shoot Weddings, Pre-Weddings, Portfolios, and all kinds of events all over the world.Our style is a blend of modern portraitures and Cinematic Films giving you a diverse collection you will treasure in your heart forever.


What we do

We specialise in Wedding photography and Films, Pre-wedding Photography and Films, often travelling to your destination to capture the perfect moments at the perfect place. We offer a wide range of photo and video services to accommodate our client's requirements.

What we love

We love to showcase your story and then see you mesmerizing with joy and love for your memories that will be cherished forever. We snap each and every precious moment of the event that later pinches your soul.

What we believe

To us, Photography and Cinematography is about people being real and then letting us to paint a picture and showcase their moments to remember it forever. This is the story that matters the most: Real people; Real stories and Real moments.
